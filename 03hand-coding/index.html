The Beginning

Autumn Lani (b. 1998) is an abstract painter living and working in Salt Lake City, Utah. As a solo-traveler, her art represents her strong connection to self and perception of color. With a continuing education in visual design and color theory, Autumn's mediums range between acrylic, gouache and watercolor. Her creativity stems from her connection to the earth and being deeply rooted in herself. She loves sharing her art at farmer's markets, galleries, art markets and is excited to launch her new website this Summer 2024.
